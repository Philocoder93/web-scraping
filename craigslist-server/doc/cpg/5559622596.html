Rockville makerspace looking for volunteers (Rockville, MD)

(google map)

no pay

Attention "makers", engineers, scientists, crafters, retirees, college students, and tech buffs!

A local makerspace is starting up in the Rockville area! We are now looking for volunteers for staffing to get it going. Plans are to start operations in June.

Equipment will include 3D printers, laser cutter, CNC milling, and more as we develop.

Our group of friendly makers like you will supervise the new area, advise users about equipment, field basic questions, mentoring opportunities, and if you are up for it even lead a class or two! This is a non-profit community effort and the volunteering is unpaid. But there is lots of opportunity to meet like-minded people and be involved in a great development for Montgomery County.

If you can be part of this for just 3 or more hours on one or more days each week that would be great! Low stress environment and lots of support. Very convenient and safe location near Town Center. The space operating hours will be: Monday-Friday 3:00pm-9:00pm, Saturday 10am-5:00pm. Closed for all County Holidays and due to inclement weather, etc. We hope to gather all those who can work with us and make it a go!

Feel free to respond with your interest, available time, or questions.
