SEO Specialist

(google map)

compensation: DOE


The diversity of our operations has recently created an opportunity for a Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Specialist in our Lorton, Virginia office.

Position Description

The Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Specialist is responsible for managing the SEO channel, local search efforts, and for managing onsite search. This role drives both strategic direction and planning for the SEO channel and owns the timely execution and evaluation of initiatives to driving sales. This role will develop and implement new tactics to drive growth and to maximize the natural search channel as a whole. The candidate will have to make difficult decisions, creatively think outside the box, and work through multiple projects, with results-driven goals at the top of mind and a past history of success. This role reports to the Online Marketing Manager.

A key objective of the role is to support the development and implementation of business innovations that will deliver incremental growth and sales.



Key Accountabilities:

Manage and develop the SEO program to ensure that year over year growth in demand and other business goals are met.
Manage and optimize onsite search for the client's website to ensure relevant results and a positive user experience to assist in driving sales.
Create, track, and manage budget for SEO.
Oversee and analyze current SEO initiatives while identifying and implement new ones.
Ensure on-page optimization best practice implementation. Manage implementation and maintenance of: title & meta tags, URL redirects, 404 errors, robots.txt file., & Google Search Console parameters
Monitor Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools.
Work with development team to ensure correct implementation of SEO recommendations and best practices and to understand areas of improvement.
Work closely with Marketing, Web Development and Creative team to support new site initiatives.
Work with the copy and content team to provide copy best practices for product descriptions, on-page copy and site content to be developed. Work with the team to manage SEO best practices style guide and keyword reference document to be used by copy team.
Work with analytics to ensure proper insights into SEO business are being measured and reported.
Work with analytics to A/B test assets to engage users on product pages that drive high traffic, but low conversions.
Work with the social media team to provide best practices and ensure maintenance of best practices.
Assist Store Operations and the International Marketing teams with local search and to manage online local listings for store locations.
Work with all relevant teams to develop and expand our international presence on the web using known best practices and methodologies.
Provide weekly performance reports on SEO channel. Work with SEO agency on creating monthly and event performance reports. Present quarterly reports to managers.
Perform advanced keyword research involving intent modeling, semantics, and competitive analysis. This includes managing keywords and categories within the Searchlight platform.
Work with SEM role to further integrate SEO & SEM to improve performance and drive sales.
Identify content gaps on site, and areas of opportunity for optimization
Other: Share SEO current events and search engine algorithm changes with internal colleagues, monitor the competitive and vendor landscape for trends and best practices, manage new program development that will lead to business growth.
Skills & Requirements

Required:

3-5 years SEO experience with an e-commerce/online retail company
Experience in international SEO
Strong knowledge of coding, search engine algorithms, and website architecture from an SEO perspective with the ability to provide top notch recommendations.
Solid analytical skills with key performance metrics
SEO knowledge of various platforms, on desktop, mobile and tablet
Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools experience
Management experience including managing external agencies in natural search efforts.
Schema.org, Facebook Open Graph, Twitter Card, and Bazaarvoice familiarity and page markup best practices.
Experience in local listings management and citations.
Good understanding of usability and online shopping behavior as our customers and online users are our number one priority.
Demonstrated ability to lead; excellent communication skills; and ability to multi-task in a fast paced environment, switching focus as necessary to accommodate deadlines and priorities.
Demandware platform knowledge is a plus.
Experience with Conductor Searchlight is a plus.
Experience with Jira CMS is a plus.
Wordpress knowledge is a plus.
