Computer Repair Technician Internship - Benefits Provided (Alexandria)

North Van Dorn at Kenmore Ave

(google map)

no pay

IQ Computer Services is looking for a computer technician intern who is interested in pursuing an opportunity to support and assist our Information Technology company with a wide range of projects related to our internal processes and client support. You will learn and develop skills in customer services, computer troubleshooting, networking and security. You will interact with your supervisor and other staff on how to improve documentation and planning, functionality and design. You will also have the ability to work independently. This internship is designed to be both educational and practical. The computer technician intern will learn how to take his or her knowledge and skills from the classroom and directly apply them to a production environment.

You Will Learn How To:
● Repair Laptop and Desktop PCs and Mac
● Create work orders, sales receipts and other documents.
● Capture client information and product specifications
● Network desktop, laptops and services, and enable new capabilities for the existing networks
● Secure data from loss using encryption and data backup and replication

What We Are Looking For:
● Strong passion for technology and learning
● Above average knowledge of computer systems and productivity software
● Basic knowledge of internet and research techniques
● Practical knowledge of networking principles
● Experience not required
● Enrolled college student or recent graduate majoring in computer science or networking

IQ Computer Services is dedicated to ensuring a mutually beneficial relationship with its interns. We value all of our interns and go the extra mile to make sure you receive a learning experience that fits your career interests and goals. You will gain a better understanding of the role that computer technology and support and the internet play in the efficiency of business organizations. Interns are also provided opportunities to learn more about the organization, our department leaders and other professionals in the information technology industry, and enhance your professional development through web-based training from industry leaders such as Microsoft, Intel, Western Digital and Symantec.

Internship Details:
This internship begins May 1, 2016 and requires a 8-week commitment. IQ Computer Services internships require a minimum of 20 hours per week and interns may not be in the office more than 8 hours per day. The internship is located in Alexandria, VA, 15 minutes from DC.

Compensation:
● Part-time (20-30 hrs. per week) $50 per month stipend
● Full-time (30-40): $100 per month stipend
● Local travel expenses are also reimbursed, up to a maximum of $20 per day

Application Instructions:
To apply for this internship, please send the following materials to show contact info . Only applications received via email will be considered for this position. Specify "Computer Technician Intern" and your name in the subject line of your email. Phone calls are not accepted prior to submission!
● Resume or C.V.
● Cover letter, stating the basis for your interest in this position and why your qualifications make you the right candidate for this internship
● Two References (names and contact information only)
