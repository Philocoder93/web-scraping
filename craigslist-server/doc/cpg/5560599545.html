Sumer Intern (Rosslyn)

11611 N Kent St

(google map)

compensation: $12 and between 10 and 20 hours a week

About RTCM:
RTCM is an international non-profit scientific, professional and educational organization. RTCM members are organizations (not individuals) that are both non-government and government. Although started in 1947 as a U.S. government advisory committee, RTCM is now an independent organization supported by its members from all over the world. Our Special Committees provide a forum in which government and non-government members work together to develop technical standards and consensus recommendations in regard to issues of with respect to the Maritime environment.

About the Internship:
RTCM is looking hiring an intern to assist with a range of communications and administrative activities for the months of June-August 2016, with an option for the internship to be extended. The internship offers an hourly rate of $12 and between 10 and 20 hours a week. The position is based at RTCM's office in Rosslyn, just two blocks from the Rosslyn Metro on the Silver, Blue or Orange Line.

Specific duties may include:
• Assist with management of the RTCM's website, including posting new content, reformatting content, applying tags, and reporting metrics
• Facilitating special committee meetings at RTCM's headquarters
• Manage the issuing of RTCM's standards and publications
• Research and create an Excel database of organizations that would be interested in RTCM's reports with tabs to sort by specific topic areas


Qualifications:
• Detail oriented
• Experience with WordPress content management system or other web platform (preferred)
• Thorough command of the English language, both written and spoken
• Experience with word processing (Word), spreadsheet (Excel), and presentation (PowerPoint) applications, as well as Internet proficiency, are required
• The ability to work independently and the flexibility to handle multiple tasks
• Previous office experience is preferred

How to Apply:
Please email you cover letter, resume and three professional references in one document
